Questionnaire

What is the largest desert in the world?
The Antarctic Desert is the largest desert in the world, covering around 14 million square kilometers.
Which is the longest river in the world?
The Nile River is considered the longest river in the world, stretching about 6,650 kilometers (4,130 miles).
Which country has the most natural lakes?
Canada has the most natural lakes in the world, with around 31,752 large lakes.
